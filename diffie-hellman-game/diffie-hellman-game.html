Diffie-Hellman-Game

Instructions

  1. Pick a PRIVATE key k that is 1 ≤ k ≤ 21
  2. Calculate Pself. This is your PUBLIC key. Anyone can see this.
  3. Calculate s, which is the shared key between you and your partner.
  4. Check to make sure you and your partner now have the same s!
  5. You have successfully shared a key. Now you can encrpyt messages without a 3rd part knowing.
