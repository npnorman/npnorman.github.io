Tic-Tac-Die

Player X's turn

Start by Placing


Rules

How to play:

  1. Each player starts by placing one tile on the board.
  2. Players take turns until win condition is met.
  3. On your turn: Roll die, follow move given.

Win Condition: If a player has 4 or more of their tokens sequentially in a row, column, or diagonal, they win. If all board spaces are filled without a match of 4 or more, the game ends in a draw.

Place The player places a piece on the board in a space of the 5x5 grid.
Swap The player swaps one of their piece's position with the opponent.
Move The player moves either their own or the opponent's piece to an empty space
Super Move The player moves either their own or the opponent's piece to an empty space, then takes another turn.
